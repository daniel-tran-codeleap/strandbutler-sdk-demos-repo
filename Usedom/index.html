Strandkorb Ahlbeck
★★★★★ (48)
Classic Baltic beach chairs on the white sands of Ahlbeck pier

The historic Ahlbeck pier and the wide imperial-resort beach are the backdrop for a relaxed day in a Strandkorb. Each hand-woven two-seater shelters you from wind and sun, with a footrest, sun canopy and a lockable storage drawer.

Rentals run daily through the season directly on the West Beach, a few steps from the promenade and the Kaiserbäder of Ahlbeck, Heringsdorf and Bansin.
