Hotel Meersinn Binz at a glance

Holiday in the heart of Binz — look forward to the sandy Baltic beach just a few steps from the house. The refreshing sea air helps you forget everyday life, while the eye delights in the grand Binz resort architecture into which the Hotel Meersinn fits as elegantly as it does charmingly.

Spacious rooms, a wellness area with sauna and indoor pool, and an à-la-carte restaurant round off your stay directly on the promenade.
